Game Rules

  1. You can move 1 to 3 spaces vertically and horizontally.
  2. Collect weapons to increase your damage score when attacking the opponent.
  3. You can attack or defend. Defending will cause you half the damage, when your opponent attacks, but will give no damage to your opponent.
  4. When you are next to your opponent you can attack or defend.
  5. The first player to have a score of zero loses.
